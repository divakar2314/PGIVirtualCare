About Us

Best Medical Care for Yourself and Your Family

PGI Virtual Care is an innovative telemedicine initiative developed to make quality healthcare accessible to everyone, regardless of their location. It offers convenient features such as online appointment booking, real-time health monitoring, and digital medical records. The intuitive and user-friendly design makes it easy for patients of all ages to navigate. PGI Virtual Care reduces the need for physical hospital visits while maintaining the highest standards of medical care and confidentiality. Developed under the guidance of Softpro Group of Institutions (SPGI), Lucknow — a premier educational institution known for excellence in engineering, management, and innovation — PGI Virtual Care is part of its mission to integrate technology with social good. With a strong team of experienced doctors, tech developers, and medical professionals, SPGI ensures that this healthcare platform aligns with the highest standards. SPGI Lucknow promotes industry-oriented skill development and is committed to nurturing projects that serve both rural and urban communities. PGI Virtual Care is a proud product of this vision, leveraging cloud-based infrastructure and real-time analytics to offer uninterrupted, accessible, and secure healthcare solutions. Currently, PGI Virtual Care serves hundreds of patients across Uttar Pradesh, connecting users with qualified doctors through virtual consultations and providing timely support for common medical concerns.
