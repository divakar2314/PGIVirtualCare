About PGI Virtual Care

About Us

Best Medical Care for Yourself and Your Family

PGI Virtual Care is an innovative telemedicine initiative developed to make quality healthcare accessible to everyone, regardless of their location. It offers convenient features such as online appointment booking, real-time health monitoring, and digital medical records. The intuitive and user-friendly design makes it easy for patients of all ages to navigate. PGI Virtual Care reduces the need for physical hospital visits while maintaining the highest standards of medical care and confidentiality.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To provide equitable and accessible healthcare using digital transformation, breaking geographical and social barriers through virtual care solutions.

Our Mission

To enhance healthcare delivery using modern technologies like AI, cloud, and IoT, enabling patients to consult doctors remotely and securely through a comprehensive web-based platform.

Benefits of PGI Virtual Care

For Patients

Access expert care from remote areas, save time and cost, and manage all records online securely.

For Doctors

Efficient scheduling, centralized access to patient data, and reduced paperwork for better productivity.

For Government

Supports Digital India initiatives, reduces hospital crowding, and promotes inclusive healthcare growth.

Challenges Without PGI Virtual Care

Long Waits

Traditional visits cause delays and long queues in hospitals.

Limited Access

Patients in rural areas struggle to reach expert medical care.

Paper Records

Records on paper are prone to errors, loss, and inefficiency.

Lack of Continuity

Patients face poor follow-ups and disconnection with doctors.
